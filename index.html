Understanding Backlinks

Backlinks, also known as inbound links, are crucial for SEO. They are created when one site links to another, signaling the quality and relevance of the linked content to search engines like Google.

How Backlinks Enhance SEO

Search engine crawlers follow the links on a page to discover new content. If a static page links to another site, it counts as a backlink. The value of a backlink depends on the quality and relevance of the linking page. High-quality backlinks from authoritative sites significantly benefit SEO.
